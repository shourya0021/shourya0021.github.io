Famous Dishes

Jowar Bhakri

A traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour served with spicy curries.

Shenga Chutney

A dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts garlic and spices.

Misal Pav

A popular Maharashtrian street food made with spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread.

Puran Poli

A sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils prepared during festivals.
